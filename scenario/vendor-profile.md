# Vendor Profile — Northwind Payroll Services

## Overview

This document describes the third-party vendor evaluated in this vendor risk assessment. The vendor provides a cloud-based payroll and human resources (HR) platform used to manage payroll processing, tax documentation, and employee information.

Because the service involves sensitive employee and financial data, the vendor underwent a formal vendor risk assessment before the organization proceeded with implementation.

---

## Organization Background

The organization conducting this assessment is **Burn and Churn Coffee**, a small coffee shop operating from a single location.

The business handles several types of sensitive information, including:

- Employee payroll and tax records
- Customer payment transactions
- Operational business data

As part of its governance and risk management practices, vendors that process sensitive data or support critical business functions are subject to a formal vendor risk assessment.

---

## Vendor Description

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Vendor Name | Northwind Payroll Services |
| Vendor ID | V-002 |
| Service Type | Cloud Payroll and HR Management Platform (SaaS) |

The platform is used for payroll processing, employee onboarding, tax form management, direct-deposit management, and employee information storage. It is accessed through a web-based interface and runs on the vendor's cloud infrastructure.

---

## Data Processed by the Vendor

- Employee personally identifiable information (PII)
- Payroll records
- Tax documentation
- Direct-deposit banking information

Because this information is sensitive and financial in nature, the vendor relationship introduces elevated risk that must be evaluated.

---

## System Access Considerations

The platform integrates with the company's internal payroll workflow and is accessed by internal HR and finance personnel. Vendor support personnel may require limited administrative access for platform maintenance.

This introduces potential risks related to:

- Privileged system access
- Employee data protection
- Operational dependency on the vendor platform

---

## Reason for Assessment

The assessment evaluates the security, operational, and compliance risks of using the payroll platform, reviewing the inherent risk introduced by the vendor relationship, the security controls the vendor maintains, and any gaps that require mitigation. The results inform the decision to approve, conditionally approve, or reject the vendor relationship.
