# Vendor Profile

## Overview

This document describes the third-party vendor(s) being evaluated as part of this vendor risk assessment.

The vendor provides a cloud-based payroll and human resources (HR) platform used to manage employee payroll processing, tax documentation, and employee information.

Because the service involves sensitive employee data and financial information, the vendor must undergo a vendor risk assessment before the organization proceeds with implementation.

---

## Organization Background

The organization conducting this assessment is a small coffee shop company that operates in one building.

The business handles several types of sensitive information including:

- Employee payroll and tax records
- Customer payment transactions
- Operational business data

To support internal operations, the company relies on several external vendors that provide technology services.

As part of the organization's governance and risk management practices, vendors that process sensitive data or support critical business functions are subject to a formal vendor risk assessment.

---

## Vendor Description

Vendor Name: **Northwind Payroll Services**

Service Type: **Cloud Payroll and HR Management Platform**

The vendor provides a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform used for:

- Payroll processing
- Employee onboarding
- Tax form management
- Direct deposit management
- Employee information storage

The platform is accessed through a web-based interface and is managed by the vendor's cloud infrastructure.

---

## Data Processed by the Vendor

The vendor will process and store sensitive employee information including:

- Employee personally identifiable information (PII)
- Payroll records
- Tax documentation
- Direct deposit banking information

Because this information is sensitive and financial in nature, the vendor relationship introduces elevated risk that must be evaluated.

---

## System Access Considerations

The vendor platform will integrate with the company's internal payroll workflow and will be accessed by internal HR and finance personnel.

Vendor support personnel may also require limited administrative access to assist with platform maintenance and support.

This introduces potential risks related to:

- privileged system access
- employee data protection
- operational dependency on the vendor platform

---

## Reason for Assessment

The vendor risk assessment is conducted to evaluate the potential security, operational, and compliance risks associated with using the payroll platform.

The assessment will review:

- the inherent risk introduced by the vendor relationship
- security controls maintained by the vendor
- any gaps or concerns that require mitigation

The results of the assessment will inform the final decision to approve, conditionally approve, or reject the vendor relationship.
