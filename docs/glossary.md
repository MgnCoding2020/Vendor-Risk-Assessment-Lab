# Vendor Risk Assessment Glossary

This glossary is to help explain key terms used throughout the **Vendor-Risk_assessment-Lab**.

The definitions are written in practical terms to support understanding of the vendor risk assessment process.

----

## Vendor
A third-party company that could provide services, software, or support to the organization.

Vendors can introduce risk by processing data, accessing systems, or supporting operations.

---

## Third-Party Risk
Risk that's introduced to the business through external companies that provide services or products.

This risk could involve, but is not limited to, data exposure, operational disruption, or a security weakness in the vendor's environment.

---

## Vendor Intake
This is the **initial** step in the vendor risk process where information about the vendor and their services provided is collected.

This helps determine the appropriate level of vendor risk review required based on the inherent risk.
